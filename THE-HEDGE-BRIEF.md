# PART ONE: WHAT WE'RE BUILDING

## 1. The Product

The Hedge is a family learning platform. The name comes from Ireland's hedge schools — the illegal open-air schools of the 18th century where families took education into their own hands when the system wouldn't serve them. That spirit of independence, resourcefulness, and learning by nature is the soul of everything we build.

The Hedge serves two audiences with one product:

- **Every family (mainstream):** Parents whose children attend school but who want screen-free ideas, weekend activities, holiday plans, and enrichment beyond homework. The app that answers "What should we do with the kids today?"
- **Homeschool families:** Parents educating at home who need a complete operating system — curriculum engine, daily plans, Tusla AEARS compliance documentation, progress tracking, and AI-powered guidance.

The front door is wide open to everyone. The homeschool features are the deep product for power users. The entry point is a universal parenting need: meaningful, screen-free time with your children.

### One-Line Pitch
"The Hedge tells you what to do with your kids today — personalised to their ages, the weather, and what you did yesterday. For homeschool families, it's the complete education system Ireland has been missing."

### Brand Voice & Personality

- **Tone:** Conversational, encouraging, practical. Like a text from your most resourceful friend. Never corporate, never preachy.
- **Visual:** Natural greens, warm earth tones, hand-drawn illustration style. Feels like a nature journal, not a tech product.
- **Language:** Irish English. "Jumper" not "sweater." "Mam" not "mom." "Grand" not "great." Sprinkle Irish words naturally: "Today's spraoi" (fun).
- **Anti-patterns:** Never guilt-trip about screen time. Never use "optimise" or "hack" about children. Never gamify in addictive ways (no streaks, no points, no leaderboards for kids).

## 2. Who It's For

### Primary Persona: The Intentional Parent
Age 28–45. One or more children aged 3–12. Lives in Ireland (expanding to UK, then US). Concerned about screen time. Values outdoor play, creativity, and hands-on learning. Feels guilty about iPad time. Wants to do better but is exhausted. Willing to pay for something that genuinely makes family time easier and better.

### Secondary Persona: The Homeschool Parent
Subset of above who has pulled children from mainstream school or is considering it. Needs everything above plus: structured curriculum, daily planning, Tusla legal compliance, progress tracking, and confidence they're providing an education at least as good as school.

### Market Size
- Ireland (mainstream): ~300,000 families with primary-age children
- Ireland (homeschool): 3,500+ registered, growing 20%+ per year
- UK: 12 million families. 80,000–100,000 homeschooling
- US: 33 million families. 3.7 million homeschooled

## 3. User Journeys

### Mainstream Family Journey

**Discovery:** Sarah sees an Instagram reel of a family doing a kitchen science experiment. Caption says "Today's idea from @thehedge.ie." She downloads the app.

**Onboarding (2 minutes):** Wizard asks: How many kids? Ages? What do they love? (visual icons). Outdoor space? When do you need ideas? She picks "after school" and "weekends." Instantly gets personalised ideas.

**First Use:** 3:30pm Tuesday. Opens app. "It's 14°C and dry in Cork. Here's what to do today." Three cards: garden scavenger hunt (4yo), kitchen measurement challenge (7yo), family fairy house build. She taps fairy house. Full instructions, no materials to buy, 30 minutes. They do it. Photo. One-tap log. Done.

**Habit:** Within a week, opening daily. Thursday push: "Weekend forecast: dry Saturday, rain Sunday. Here's your plan." Upgrades to Family (€6.99/mo).

### Homeschool Family Journey

**Discovery:** Emma homeschools two kids (6, 9). Stressed about Tusla assessment. Googles "how to prepare for Tusla homeschool assessment Ireland." Finds The Hedge's guide. Signs up.

**Onboarding (15 min):** Extended wizard: children's details, approach (blended), priorities, hours, Tusla status (awaiting assessment). The Hedge generates complete yearly plan for both children mapped to Irish curriculum.

**Daily Use:** Opens app. "Monday — Oisín (9): Maths (fractions with pizza, 30 min), English (creative writing, 30 min), Nature (bird ID walk, 45 min). Aoife (6): Phonics (letter blends, 20 min), Maths (counting with buttons, 20 min), Art (paint a flower, 30 min). Together: Irish vocab game (15 min)."

**Tusla Assessment:** 3 weeks before, taps "Prepare for Assessment." Complete portfolio PDF generated: curriculum coverage, sample work, progress notes, education plan. Walks in confident. Assessor impressed.

## 4. Complete Feature Specification

### 4.1 Mobile App (iOS & Android) — The Daily Companion

- **Today's Ideas:** 3–5 personalised activity cards. Based on children's ages, weather (Open-Meteo API), season, time, recent history, family prefs. Each card: title, time, materials (household), age range, learning outcomes, mess level, energy level.
- **Quick filters:** "15 minutes" / "Raining" / "In the car" / "No mess" / "Calm" / "Active" / "Screen-free"
- **Activity detail:** Full instructions, steps, materials checklist, tips, extensions, timer
- **One-tap logging:** Done button. Optional photo, notes, rating. Feeds timeline + portfolio.
- **Screen-free mode:** Activities as iPad/TV alternatives. Categorised by energy level.
- **Weekend planner:** Thursday push with weather-based weekend plan. Editable.
- **Holiday planner:** Week-by-week plans for mid-term, Christmas, Easter, summer.
- **AI chat:** "We're at the beach with a 4 and 7 year old." Instant contextual suggestions.
- **Push notifications:** Morning idea (configurable). Weather triggers. Weekly summary. Max 1/day.
- **Offline mode:** Today's activities cached.
- **Calendar sync:** Apple Calendar, Google Calendar.
- **Family timeline:** Scrollable feed with photos. Memory log + homeschool evidence.

### 4.2 Web Platform (thehedge.ie) — The Command Centre

- **Full content library:** Browse, search, filter all activities
- **Favourites & collections:** Custom groups ("Rainy days", "Car journeys")
- **Family dashboard:** Recent activity, patterns, seasonal suggestions
- **Educator suite (Educator tier):** Full planning — yearly/termly/weekly/daily. Curriculum alignment. Drag-and-drop.
- **Tusla compliance centre (Educator tier):** Application guide, plan generator, portfolio, coverage dashboard, assessment prep.
- **Community hub:** County groups, forums, events, co-ops, mentors, swaps.
- **AI assistant:** Full conversational with family context.

### 4.3 iPad App — The Child's Space

- **My Activities:** Visual, age-appropriate view. Icons for young children, list for older.
- **Interactive exercises:** Maths, phonics, reading, science. Curriculum-aligned, adaptive.
- **Creative space:** Drawing, writing, journaling. Saves to portfolio.
- **Reading corner:** Book recommendations + library links.
- **Achievement garden:** Gentle visual progress. No points/leaderboards.
- **Parental controls:** Time limits, content restrictions. No external links, no YouTube, no social.

## 6. Curriculum Engine (Educator Tier)

Maps to NCCA Primary Curriculum Framework: Language (English/Irish), Mathematics, Science & Technology, Social & Environmental Education, Arts, PE, SPHE/Wellbeing. Four stages: Stage 1 (JI/SI), Stage 2 (1st/2nd), Stage 3 (3rd/4th), Stage 4 (5th/6th). Flexes to child's ability.

### Tusla AEARS Compliance
Tusla assesses "certain minimum education" covering: intellectual development (literacy, numeracy, other learning), social/emotional development, moral development, physical development. Under Section 14, Education (Welfare) Act 2000.

The Hedge automates:
- **Application guidance:** Step-by-step AEARS form walkthrough
- **Education plan document:** Auto-generated PDF
- **Portfolio builder:** Evidence from daily logs — photos, work samples, progress notes
- **Coverage dashboard:** Traffic light (green/amber/red) by curriculum area
- **Assessment prep:** What to expect, checklist, AI review 3 weeks before
- **Attendance record:** Auto-logged from plan completion
- **Progress reports:** Termly/annual, auto-generated

## 11. AI Integration (Claude API)

### Activity Suggestion Engine
- **System prompt:** "You are The Hedge, a family learning companion inspired by Ireland's hedge schools. Suggest activities based on children's ages, interests, weather, season, time available, and recent history. Respond with structured JSON."
- **Context:** Children's ages, interests, SEN flags, family style, weather (Open-Meteo), season, time, duration, recent 14-day log, county.
- **Output:** JSON array of 5 suggestions: {title, description, duration, materials, steps, learning_outcomes, age_suitability, why_today}
- **Fallback:** If API unavailable, serve from activity DB using tag matching.

### Curriculum Plan Generation (Educator)
- **Input:** Child profile, stage, approach, priorities, hours/days, interests, SEN, curriculum outcomes.
- **Output:** Termly plan with weekly themes and daily blocks mapped to outcomes.

### Rate Limits
- Free: 5 queries/week
- Family: 30/week
- Educator: Unlimited
- Cost: ~$0.003/request

## 14. Security & Privacy

- **RLS:** Every table has Row Level Security. Families only access their own data.
- **Auth:** Supabase handles sessions, JWTs, hashing, rate limiting.
- **API:** All routes verify JWT. Educator routes check subscription.
- **AI:** API key server-side only. Rate limited per tier. Zero-retention.
- **Storage:** Supabase Storage with RLS. Family-scoped buckets.
- **GDPR:** Data export + deletion. Cookie consent. EU data residency.
- **Child safety:** No child accounts. No external links in iPad app. No social for children. Children First Act aligned.
